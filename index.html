Quick Tips

Lock colors: Click the lock icon on any swatch to preserve it during regeneration.
Drag to reorder: Grab the grip handle on swatches to manually arrange your palette.
Tints & shades: Double-click any color swatch to see its full spectrum.
Copy format: Use the HEX/RGB/HSL pills to change default copy behavior. Shift+click for RGB, Alt+click for HSL.
Color blindness: Toggle simulation modes above the live preview to check accessibility.
Compare: Save palettes to favorites, then use the Compare button to see contrast grids.
